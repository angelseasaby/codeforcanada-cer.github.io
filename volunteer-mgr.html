The National Energy Board (NEB) is committed to protecting the privacy rights of individuals.

The definition of “personal information” is any information, in any form, about an identifiable individual. Refer to section 3 of the Privacy Act for further details regarding personal information.

The personal information you provide will be used by the National Energy Board for research and development purposes; and is collected under the authority of the Financial Administration Act, and in accordance with the Communications Policy of the Government of Canada (https://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=12316) and is described in the Standard personal information banks (PSU 914 - Public Communications): https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/access-information-privacy/access-information/information-about-programs-information-holdings/standard-personal-information-banks.html#psu914. Provision of your information is voluntary, and will not be used to make any administrative decisions about you.

Personal information is collected and used as described in the Treasury Board Secretariat’s Standard Personal Information Bank Outreach Activities (PSU 938)

Individuals have the right to file a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner of Canada regarding the department’s handling of their personal information.


About this Application


We appreciate your interest in helping to develop a more modern and accessible regulatory database. Please read the privacy notice stated above and click on the button below, it will open a form for you to apply to participate.

Inclusivity and accessibility are very important to us. As part of our process, we aim to collect information on, and speak to, a pool of participants that is diverse and representative of the people who reside in Canada. This information will be kept confidential and will only be used to ensure equitable participation.
